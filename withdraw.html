Bank overview / Payment Process

Making a Purchase (Deposit)

  1. Pokerdhamaal.com offers a variety of ways to deposit money using credit cards, bank cards, Net Banking, etc. All of our payment methods are easy to use, efficient, and completely secure. Once you have paid by any payment method, you will be able to play our exciting cash games.
  2. To ensure the highest security for our customers, we only process cards with two-factor authentication to prevent Internet fraud. In addition, card details are entered by the customer directly at a trusted payment gateway worldwide. It is not maintained in any way on our site thus ensuring complete security and peace of mind.

You can buy in 4 easy steps:

  1. Step 1: Click on Deposite in the "Lobby" section (after creating your account with us)
  2. Step 2: Select a value (in INR) from the drop-down list. Select payment method (Credit Card / Debit Card / Netbanking / Online Wallet / Pay via UPI) and click to continue.
  3. Step 3: Complete the transaction at the payment gateway
  4. Step 4: You will automatically return to our site once you have completed the process. You can immediately see your Wallet balance renewed at the "Account Summary" link in the "My Account" section. You will also receive an email from your registered email ID showing the transaction value and confirming the success of the transaction.
  5. Complying with RBI procedures, we do not accept international cards on the site.

Withdrawal Policy:

  1. At Pokerdhamaal.com, we have a rescue policy that suits customers.
  2. All values ​​you choose to use will be deducted immediately from your PokerDhamaal account. It will no longer be available for play.
  3. Minimum amount of Rs 200.KYC verification is mandatory for users requesting redemption.
  4. If you have any questions, please refer to our FAQ section or Contact Us for more details.

The following Tax policy applies to PokerDhamaal websites and services (collectively "Platform") run by Gworld Gaming Private Limited ("Company") All withdrawals from the Platform are subject to tax deduction (TDS)in terms of applicable amounts (currently. 30% (plus applicable) for withdrawals of more than Rs. 10,000) submitted to Indian tax authorities under section 194B of the Income Tax Act. All withdrawals from the Platform will be calculated for the user's TDS debt and TDS will only be deducted from the relevant withdrawal, i.e., withdrawal within 24 hours (from IST 4:00 PM on one day to IST 3:59 PM the next day) which is added to the amount in excess of Rs. 10,000. Platform withdrawal requests will be processed on Anytime 24*7
